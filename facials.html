What Does the Dermaplaning Process Look Like?
A sterile surgical blade is used to carefully and safely exfoliate the top layer of the skin's surface.
This process removes the barrier of dead skin and fine hair, which often traps dirt and oil. By removing this layer, we prep your complexion to fully absorb the nourishing benefits of ElaSpa serums, resulting in deep nourishment and a glass-like finish.
Skin feels instantly smoother, looks brighter, and is perfectly prepped for makeup application. The treatment is painless, with no downtime, and can be done every 3-4 weeks for optimal results.

FAQs
  • For optimal skin health and to support cell turnover, one treatment every 4-6 weeks is recommended.
  • Stop using Retin-A, retinols, or high-strength acids 3-5 days prior. Avoid direct sun exposure for 7 days, and do not shave or wax just before.
  • It is best to wait 24 hours to let your pores breathe and prevent clogging after deep cleansing.
  • It is common for skin to purge or break out 1-2 days after, as extractions release congestion.
  • Arrive with a clean, makeup-free face, and be ready to discuss your current skincare routine, allergies, and goals with the esthetician.
  • Keep your skin hydrated, use gentle products, and apply sunscreen to protect freshly exfoliated skin.
