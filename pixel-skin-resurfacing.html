Pixel Skin Resurfacing
Revitalize Texture & Tone with Minimal Downtime
Reveal smoother, younger-looking skin with resurfacing technology
Pixel Skin Resurfacing is an advanced aesthetic laser treatment that lifts away years from your face. It stimulates new collagen growth to improve skin texture and tone, fighting fine lines, wrinkles, and even acne scars.
This fractional laser technology creates thousands of microscopic perforations, which minimizes downtime compared to traditional resurfacing. The intact skin around these "pixels" aids in rapid healing, allowing you to return to your routine faster.
We offer this treatment for those looking to reclaim a youthful glow, smooth out rough skin, and reduce the appearance of pores and pigmentation. It is a safe and effective way to restore your skin's natural radiance.

How Does Pixel Resurfacing Work?
The Pixel laser creates thousands of microscopic "thermal zones" or perforations in the skin, removing the epidermis and upper dermis. Because the laser leaves bridges of untouched skin between these perforations, healing is much faster than traditional ablative lasers.
This "fractional" approach allows your skin to heal from the inside out. The micro-injury triggers the body's natural healing process, stimulating the production of new collagen and elastin.
As the skin heals, imperfections are replaced by fresh, smooth, and radiant skin tissue. Over time, this results in firmer skin, reduced wrinkles, and a clearer complexion.
