How Do PCA Peels Work?
PCA Peels use a carefully formulated blend of acids to gently remove the outer layers of dead skin cells. This process stimulates natural exfoliation and accelerates skin renewal.
By removing the dull, damaged outer layer, the treatment encourages collagen production and improves elasticity.
The removal of dead cells also allows active ingredients to penetrate the skin more effectively, enhancing overall skin health and radiance.
FAQs
  • Clients may experience mild redness, tingling, or a warming sensation during the application. These sensations are normal and subside quickly.
  • Depending on the specific peel type and your skin concern, downtime can range from none to a few days. You may experience light peeling or flaking for a few days after treatment as new skin is revealed.
  • Results often appear after the first session, with skin looking more radiant and refreshed. Texture and tone will continue to improve as the skin heals.
  • While you will see a glow after one peel, a series of treatments is typically recommended for maximum improvement in texture, tone, and overall clarity.
  • PCA Peels are versatile and can treat acne, acne scars, hyperpigmentation, sun damage, fine lines, and uneven texture.
