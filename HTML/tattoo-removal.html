How Does It Work?
The laser emits short pulses of high-intensity energy that target the pigment in the tattoo. These pulses break the tattoo ink into tiny, microscopic particles.
Once the ink is shattered, the bodyâ€™s immune system naturally flushes these particles away over time. Because the laser targets the pigment specifically, the surrounding skin remains unharmed.
Different ink colors absorb laser energy differently, which is why multiple sessions are required. The laser settings are adjusted to target the specific depth and color of your tattoo for optimal removal.
FAQs
  • During treatment, clients may feel a mild snapping or tingling sensation, often described as a rubber band snapping against the skin. We can apply a topical numbing cream to ensure your comfort.
  • Some redness or mild swelling may occur immediately after the session, but this typically resolves within a few hours. You can return to your normal activities shortly after.
  • Multiple treatments are needed for optimal fading. The exact number depends on the ink density, color, and size of the tattoo. We will provide a customized treatment plan during your consultation.
  • Treatments are spaced several weeks apart. This time is essential to allow your body's immune system to eliminate the broken-down ink particles before the next session.
  • The Alma Harmony laser is designed to treat a wide range of ink colors. While black ink is the easiest to remove, our technology effectively targets colored inks as well.
