Biomicroneedling
Natural Spongilla Spicule Treatment (Face & Body)
Natural Skin Rejuvenation Without the Machine
Spongilla Spicule Treatments use natural, microscopic freshwater sponge needles to create gentle micro-channels in the skin. This stimulates collagen production, boosts cell turnover (accelerating the cycle from ~28 days to ~7 days), and improves texture and tone without heat, trauma, or the use of devices.
It’s a natural alternative to chemical peels or traditional microneedling, ideal for acne, scarring, pigmentation, fine lines, and overall skin rejuvenation with minimal downtime.
Key Benefits
Improves Texture & Tone: Reduces acne, scars, dark spots, and uneven pigmentation.
Anti-Aging: Stimulates collagen and elastin for firmer, smoother, younger-looking skin.
Pore Minimization: Helps tighten enlarged pores.
Enhanced Absorption: Boosts the effectiveness of topical treatments applied afterward.
Who Can Benefit?
  • All skin types (oily, dry, combination, sensitive)
  • Acne-prone or scarred skin
  • Dull, sun-damaged, or hyperpigmented skin
  • Mature skin seeking lifting & hydration
How Does It Work?
Natural Micro-Needles: Tiny, sharp spicules from freshwater sponges are blended with a nourishing essence and gently massaged into the skin.
Stimulates Healing: The spicules create millions of microscopic channels in the dermis, prompting the skin’s natural repair and renewal process.
Rapid Cell Turnover: This treatment accelerates the skin’s regeneration cycle, revealing fresh, rejuvenated skin faster than traditional methods. Unlike traditional microneedling, this treatment is device-free and non-invasive.
FAQs
  • Typically, the treatment involves minimal discomfort. Unlike traditional microneedling, there is no machine, bleeding, or numbing required. You may feel a sensation similar to an exfoliation scrub.
  • There is minimal downtime. Because the treatment relies on natural spicules rather than heat or aggressive trauma, the healing process is usually quick and manageable.
  • Biomicroneedling is a needle-free alternative that uses natural spicules to stimulate deep skin renewal. Safe for all skin types, one session is comparable to 3-5 traditional treatments, effectively targeting active acne, scarring, and aging without bleeding or numbing.
  • Yes, it is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin. However, we always recommend a consultation to ensure it is the best fit for your specific skin condition.
  • Because this treatment accelerates the cell turnover cycle from 28 days to just 7 days, you can expect to see fresher, smoother skin much faster than with standard treatments.
